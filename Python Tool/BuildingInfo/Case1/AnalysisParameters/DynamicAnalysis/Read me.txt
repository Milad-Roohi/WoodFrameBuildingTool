Different damping options are provide

InitialRayleigh	Initial stiffness proportional damping 
TangentRayleigh Tangent stiffness proportional damping
MassRayleigh	Pure mass proportional damping
Modal		Modal damping

For modal damping model, number of modes hold be provided
